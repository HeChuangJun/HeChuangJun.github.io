Tell Us About Yourself(自我介绍) Can you give me a self-introduction within 1minute ?(自我介绍)

  • Good morning, interviewer, My name is xxx,I come from Qingyuan,a city in Guangdong Province. I was graduated from Guangdong Pharmaceutical University in 2018, majoring in pharmaceutical engineering. l have been working as a java development engineer for 6 years, and have done traditional medical projects and Internet social projects, in which I have used springboot, mybatis framework and redis distributed cache component. rabbitmq message queue, mysql database, etc. In my last job, I was responsible for core module development, participated in requirement review and database design, I hope I can make a good performance today. That’s all. Thank you for giving me the chance.

Tell me about your project

斑点猪活动圈是一个为兴趣社区活动组织者提供服务的小程序。它被用来代替微信笔记和分账单来举办活动，兴趣社区的组织者是那些管理羽毛球和篮球俱乐部的人。项目主要由活动模块、圆圈模块、金蛋模块和事件模块组成。在上一份工作中，我负责活动模块和圈模块的开发与设计，活动模块主要包括发布活动、活动参与活动、活动结束后收款等功能，圈模块主要包括创建社区圈、圈频道聊天等功能;我使用了springcloud阿里微服务，redis分布式组件，mysql数据库和rabbitmq消息队列，项目的主要亮点是支付模块的设计和Circle频道聊天的设计

The Spotted pig activity circle is a small program to provide services for Interest community activity organizers who manage badminton and basketball clubs.
It was used to replace the wechat Note and Split Bill to hold Activity.
The project mainly consists of Activity module,Circle module,Golden Egg module and Event module.
In my last job, I was responsible for the development and design of activity modules and circle modules, The Activity module includes functions such as publishing activities, event participating in activities, and receiving payment after the activities, and The Circle module includes the creation of community circles, circle channel chat and other functions,
I Had used SpringCloudAlibaba microservice,redis distributed component,mysql database and rabbitmq message queue in project.
The main highlights of the project are the design of the payment module and the Circle channel chat

  • 我已经做了6年的java开发工程师，在我的上一份工作中，我负责核心模块的开发，参与需求审查和数据库设计，从我以前的经验来看，我相信我能很好地处理这项工作，最后，我有很强的沟通能力，这将帮助我有效地融入团队，我能在压力下工作。

What are your strengths？

  • i am a quick learner that will help me quickly overcome some difficult tasks,The best proof is that I taught（塔夫） myself to become a java development engineer
  • 我是一个快速学习的人，这将帮助我迅速克服一些困难的任务.最好的证明是我通过自学成为一名java开发工程师

What are your weaknesses？

  • I am a little OCD,for example,I will check the details of the task many times, such as sending important emails,coding important module, This might be a bit of a waste of time
  • 有点强迫症，比如，我会多次检查任务的细节，比如发送重要的邮件，编码重要的模块，这可能会有点浪费时间
